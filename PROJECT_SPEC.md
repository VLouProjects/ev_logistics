# Project Specification — Electric Logistics Vehicle Solutions Platform

## Business Positioning

We are a multi-brand electric logistics vehicle solutions specialist.

We help commercial customers select, source, configure, and deploy electric logistics vehicles, including electric vans, electric trucks, and refrigerated vehicle solutions.

We are not positioning as a vehicle manufacturer.

## Core Business Capabilities

- Multi-brand electric vehicle sourcing
- Electric van and truck selection
- Refrigerated and non-refrigerated vehicle options
- Cold-chain transport configuration
- Fleet advisory and cost evaluation
- Commercial vehicle procurement support

## Website Goals

- Generate qualified B2B inquiries
- Establish expert authority
- Help customers understand vehicle options
- Present cold-chain capability as a differentiator
- Support multi-brand sourcing and advisory positioning

## Website Tone

- Expert

## Do Not

- Claim we manufacture vehicles.
- Present fixed self-owned truck models.
- Use exact ROI claims.
- Use unsupported technical claims.
- Mention specific geographic market in homepage hero.
