# Claude Code Instructions

## Project Identity

This website is for a multi-brand electric logistics vehicle solutions specialist.

The company helps commercial customers compare, source, and configure electric vans, electric trucks, and refrigerated logistics vehicle solutions.

The company is not a vehicle manufacturer.

## Core Positioning

Use this positioning consistently:

企業級電動物流車輛及冷鏈運輸方案專家

English meaning:
Enterprise-grade electric logistics vehicle and cold-chain transport solutions expert.

## Content Rules

- Use Traditional Chinese for customer-facing copy.
- Keep content region-neutral.
- Do not mention Macau, Hong Kong, Greater Bay Area, or China in homepage positioning.
- Do not describe the company as a manufacturer.
- Do not imply the company owns or produces vehicle models.
- Use language such as:
  - 多品牌車型選擇
  - 專業選型
  - 採購及配置建議
  - 車隊規劃
  - 冷鏈運輸方案
  - 商用車隊顧問

## Design Rules

- Industrial B2B aesthetic.
- Premium but practical.
- 12-column desktop grid.
- 1200px max-width.
- Use Tailwind CSS only.
- Use design tokens only.
- No 3D configurator in v1.
- No heavy animation in v1.

## Build Rules

- Keep components reusable.
- Keep business data in `/data`.
- Keep UI components in `/components`.
- Keep pages in `/app`.
- No backend in v1.
- No fake brand names.
- No guaranteed savings or ROI claims.
